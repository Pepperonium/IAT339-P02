Brand Identity

What does the company do, or offer?

Little Bee is a company that provides customized and personalized hair care and skin care products for children.

Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)

The Little Bee company is targeted to babies and children themselves as the products are designed for them. This is also targeted to parents who would like to buy gentle hair care and skin care that is suitable for kids and people who are interested in buying gifts for friends and family.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

While there are baby hair care and skin care in the market, ingredients of the products are not very transparent - it mostly includes chemicals.

The Little Bee company provides hair care and skin care that caters to children’s wants and needs. Little Bee products are only made of natural ingredients and are paraben-free, silicon-free, and sulfate-free. Being environmentally-friendly, all Little Bee products are biodegradable and recyclable. One of Little Bee’s goals is to meet the wants and needs of every single children - they provide customized and personalized hair care and skin care to cater any aesthetic and functional needs by taking quizzes to see what best fits for their children.

Fonts

Font 1: Karla

Karla is used for the company name, title, headings and subheadings.

The Kara font is chosen because it is easy to read, making it accessible for people to read, especially children. Since it is a sans-serif font, it provides a modern and clean feel which helps target a younger audience. Additionally, using it for the company name, the simple font makes it easier to distinguish and remember.


Font 2: Inconsolata

Inconsolata is used for the body.

The Inconsolata font is chosen to create contrast between the simplistic modern headings/titles. It also creates a typewriter/vintage feel which makes content more personal towards the target audience: children. The Inconsolata font letters have the same width making it easy to read since it is spaced out.

Tone and Language

When writing copy, we should be formal yet be friendly, optimistic and genuine - communicate with respect and honesty. Try to be simple and straightforward, and avoid using complicated deep terminologies as this is target towards kids, parents and anyone who may be interested in the products. Though this is targeted to a younger audience, avoid using slangs! Know when and how use standard capitalization, symbols, numbers and paragraph formats.
